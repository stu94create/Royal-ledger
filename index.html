      How to use the ledger
      1. Pick a list — tap any entry on the Contents page; the book turns to its page.
      2. Inscribe — on a list page, tap “Inscribe a new entry”, name the item, note a quantity, then press Inscribe.
      3. Seal — tap an entry to stamp it with wax once you have it.
      4. Unseal — tap a sealed entry to undo the wax.
      5. Erase — tap the small × on the right of an entry to strike it from the ledger.
      6. Sweep — clear all sealed entries at once with the link at the foot of each list.
